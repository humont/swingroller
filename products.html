The Original

The Original Junior was launched in 1999 and was designed for kids from 4 to 11 years old. Perfect for outdoor leisure, this 3 wheel cart works with foot action on almost any flat surface.

Your kids will have so much fun riding around, racing with their friends or playing hand ball while developing their physical and motor skills. Moreover, this model is sold with its extension bar, that allows your kids to ride their Swing Junior till 11 years old thanks the 2 size position!

Pick up your favorite colour ! The Original is available in red, blue, green and purple. The Original is composed of a strong welded structure, a triple velcro extra strong tarpaulin padded soft seat and steel handles that will protect your child's hands. 

The wheels are made of Urethane, which allow a greater grip

  • EN 71 tested.
  • 4 to 12 years old
  • 50 kg max 
  • Size : 98 x 43 x 28
  • Soft seat : Polyester 
  • Footstep and handle : plastic 
  • Pu wheel size : 100mm

The First

The First has been designed for kids from 4 to 9 years old. Perfect for outdoor leisure, this 3 wheels cart works with foot action on almost any flat surface. 

Your kids will have so much fun riding around, racing with their friends or playing hand ball while developing their physical and motor skills. Moreover, the adjustable seat will allow your kids to ride their First Swingroller for a longer stretch of their childhood !

Let them play in a safe and comfortable way!

Pick up your favorite colour! The First is available in red, blue, green and purple !

The First is composed of a strong welded structure, a comfortable and adjustable bucket seat with inmold handles.

The wheels are made of Urethane, which allow a greater grip.

  • EN 71 and ASTM tested.
  • Age 4 to 9 years
  • Hard seat 
  • 50 kg max
  • Front wheel PU casted 100x24 mm
  • Rear Pu casted wheels 80x24mm
